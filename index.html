Our story

Some flavours take a lifetime to get right.
Ours took three.

Lauk began the way most Malaysian food stories do — at someone's table. We spent years learning, building, and yes, failing. A pandemic closed our kitchens. What it couldn't close was the conviction that Malaysian food deserves better.

So we came back. Not with a kitchen. With a jar.

What Lauk is

More than food.
A food system.

01

Community sourced

Every ingredient traces back to a real producer — a smallholder, a cooperative, a family who has been getting it right for generations. We find them, honour them, and bring their work to the world.

02

Circular by design

No waste. By-products become new products. Packaging is recyclable. Every sen earned is reinvested back into the communities who make Lauk possible — building wealth, not dependency.

03

Radically honest

Scan the QR on any Lauk product and meet the producer. See where it was grown. Know what's in it. No mystery. No fluff. Just food with a face.
